As a Recruitment Lead you will be responsible for the overall recruitment activities at a scaling company from creating a roadmap and strategy to develop new processes and optimize them, manage a team and establish international recruitment too.
What You Will Do
  • Create and realize a recruitment strategy and road map in cooperation with business functions under the direction of the CEO/CPO according to Datapaos business strategy
  • Manage end-to-end recruitment of technical and non-technical positions from job specification to advertising, interviewing and onboarding new hires
  • Ensure a top-notch Candidate and Hiring Manager Experience
  • Advise Hiring Managers and Interviewers on selection tools, methods and interviewing techniques
  • Create and develop recruitment reports and analytics
  • Build, coordinate and mentor the recruiting team
  • Participate in creating employer branding strategy and execution of employer branding campaigns internally and externally
  • Build Datapaos professional network through relationships with recruitment agencies, universites and other partners
  • Cooperate with Datapaos Management, Hiring Managers and Interviewers, nurture relationships with Datapao's employees
Skills You Have
  • Experience in end-to-end technical and non-technical recruitment and managing recruitment processes at a scaling company from job specification to onboarding
  • Stakeholder management experience, willingness to understand business goals and needs
  • Experience in creating and optimizing recruitment processes and systems
  • Hands-on experience with ATSs and HR databases
  • Experience in and knowledge of tools and methods for creating excellent candidate experience
  • Interest in technology, ability to understand and quickly adopt to new concepts, technologies, tools etc.
  • Familiarity with the Hungarian labor law, data privacy (GDPR) and relocation regulations and other related legislations
  • Experience in developing HR Analytics and Reporting
  • Experience in managing a recruitment team
  • Empathetic, supportive, open-minded attitude
  • Excellent time management and organizational skills
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Excellent interpersonal skills
  • Fluent verbal and written English
  • Experience in scaling a recruitment organization and in social media advertising and content marketing related to employer branding are a nice to have
